Tour Overview

Your Jungle Adventure Awaits

Escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse yourself in the pristine beauty of Chitwan National Park. This carefully curated tour takes you from the vibrant capital of Kathmandu through scenic countryside to one of Nepal's most beloved wildlife destinations.

Scenic Journey

Travel in comfort on a deluxe tourist bus, winding through green countryside, tranquil villages, and picturesque forests until you arrive at Sauraha, the gateway town to Chitwan National Park.

Warm Hospitality

Upon arrival, you'll be greeted with typical warm Nepalese hospitality at an attractive resort or hotel where meals are tasty and the atmosphere is relaxed and welcoming.

Guided Exploration

Throughout your stay, enjoy participating in activities that allow exploration of the community's diversity with guided jungle walks, canoeing, and boat rides along the Rapti River.

Explore Chitwan
Highlights

Experience the Best of Chitwan

Scenic Bus Journey

Enjoy breathtaking views of hills and river landscapes during your journey from Kathmandu to Chitwan.

Jeep Safari

Drive around Chitwan National Park in our private jeep, spotting one-horned rhinoceroses, deer, crocodiles, and if you're lucky, the elusive Bengal tiger.

Rapti River Canoeing

Roam the Rapti River in peace and quiet, watching a variety of birds and gazing at crocodiles basking near your boat.

Elephant Breeding Centre

Visit the Elephant Breeding Centre and learn about the lives and care of these gentle giants.

Tharu Cultural Dance

Enjoy a mesmerizing evening Tharu cultural dance performance showcasing the vibrant traditions of the local community.

Nature Walk

Take a guided nature walk with experienced local naturalists who will introduce you to the park's fascinating flora and fauna.

Sunset Over Rapti

Enjoy a breathtaking sunset over the Rapti River - the perfect way to end your day in the jungle.

Photography Opportunities

Capture stunning wildlife photos and scenic landscapes throughout your adventure.

Itinerary

Your 3-Day Adventure

Day 1: Kathmandu → Chitwan

  • Early morning departure from Thamel, Kathmandu
  • Scenic bus journey through countryside and hills
  • Arrival at Sauraha, Chitwan
  • Welcome drink and check-in at resort/hotel
  • Lunch at the resort
  • Afternoon: Visit Elephant Breeding Centre
  • Evening: Tharu cultural dance performance
  • Dinner and overnight stay

Day 2: Full Day Jungle Activities

  • Early morning: Bird watching and nature walk
  • Breakfast at the resort
  • Jeep safari inside Chitwan National Park
  • Spotting wildlife: rhinos, deer, crocodiles, and possibly tigers
  • Lunch break
  • Afternoon: Canoe ride on Rapti River
  • Witness crocodiles and diverse bird species
  • Evening: Breathtaking sunset over Rapti River
  • Dinner and overnight stay

Day 3: Chitwan → Kathmandu

  • Early morning: Optional activities (bicycle tour, horseback riding)
  • Breakfast at the resort
  • Check-out and departure
  • Scenic bus journey back to Kathmandu
  • Arrival in Thamel, Kathmandu
  • End of tour with unforgettable memories
